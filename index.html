Our Philosophy

Sport is not just about winning.
It's about belonging.

Founded by a mother of an autistic child, Love & Fire was built on a simple premise: traditional sports leagues are often too overwhelming for neurodivergent kids. We created a sensory-friendly, low-pressure environment where your child can learn teamwork, develop motor skills, and most importantly, have fun.
