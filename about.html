Founded by a mother's dream

The Heart Behind
Love & Fire

"As a mother of a child with autism, I experienced firsthand the struggle of finding inclusive sports programs that truly understood my child."

Regular leagues were often too loud, too fast, and too overwhelming. On the other hand, specialized adaptive programs were incredibly scarce, with long waitlists and limited options. I saw my child sitting on the sidelines, wanting to play, but not having a safe space to do so.

I created Love & Fire Adaptive Sports because I wanted to build more than just a sports league—I wanted to build a community. A place where we celebrate small victories, focus on pure joy, and adapt our coaching to meet each child where they are.

JF

Jane Founder

Executive Director & Mother

The Pillars of Our Community

What We Stand For

Radical Inclusion

We don't just "accept" differences; we celebrate them. Our programs are engineered to ensure every child has a role to play.

Absolute Safety

Safety goes beyond physical. We create sensory-safe environments where children feel mentally and emotionally secure.

Focus on Joy

Our primary KPI is a smiling child. Competition takes a back seat to high-fives, laughter, and personal growth.
